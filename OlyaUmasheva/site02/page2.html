Oh, how I love this director. David Fincher >>> everyone else. I discovered him thanks to Fight Club. I first saw this film when I was 13. I found it interesting, but I didn't understand everything. And how surprised I was the first time I realized who Tyler was! I recently rewatched it and liked it even more. To be honest, in the Russian dub, the narrator seems more canonical, that tone. It's very interesting to analyze the theme that the author wanted to convey in Tyler Durden's words. The ending and its soundtrack are impressive. “It's only after we've lost everything that we're free to do anything.” Chuck Palahniuk, Fight Club.
